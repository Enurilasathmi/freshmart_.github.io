Our Loyalty Cards



Basic loyalty card
  • Earn points on every purchase.
  • Access to member-only discounts and promotions.

Features:

  • Free to join.
  • Points can be redeemed for discounts or free items.

How to Qualify:

  • Available to all customers upon signing up.
Premium Card
  • Enhanced rewards and points accumulation.
  • Exclusive offers and priority customer service.

Features:

  • Qualification based on spending threshold or loyalty tier.
  • Additional perks like birthday rewards or extended return policies.

How to Qualify:

  • Achieve a certain spending level within a specified period.

VIP card
  • Highest level of rewards and personalized service.
  • Invitation-only events and special experiences.

Features:

  • Reserved for top-spending customers or long-term loyal members.
  • Premium rewards, personalized concierge service, and custom benefits.

How to Qualify:

  • By invitation based on spending history, loyalty, or specific criteria set by the supermarket.
